User Documentation for Oratorio

What is Oratorio?

Oratorio is a web-based speech coach. Practise your speech or presentation while recording with Oratorio, and it will provide you powerful feedback and goals by analyzing your speech!

How do I use Oratorio?

The user interface is designed to be minimalistic and simple to use. Simply click the "RECORD" button on the homepage to start recording your speech. There is also a Mirror Mode toggle that allows uses your webcam as a mirror so you can see yourself while you present. Oratorio will analyze the speech and provide statistics such as word frequency, average pace, and tone-analysis. You can save these statistics to track your progress and goals for the speech by signing in with Google. Group multiple recordings together in one speech to track your progress across iterations of that speech!

How to report a bug?

Please help us improve Oratorio by reporting bugs and issues to our GitHub repository by clicking the "New Issue" button and filling in the form. A sample is provided below:

Issue: The site crashes when I click record 3 times in a row.
Steps to reproduce: Click the record button 3 times consecutively.
Expected Behavior: Record button starts recording, stops recording, then starts recording again.
Actual Behavior: Record button starts recording, stops recording, and then the site crashes.
Operation System: Mac OS X Leopard 10.5.x
Browser: Chrome
Additional Information: It does not crash on Safari.
